# Help for ~pets~

~pets~ are familiars that follow you and assist you.

They may provide you with stat bonuses, special powers, help you in combat, or carry objects for you.

When you first get a ~pet~, you can give it a name:

  ~pet name {newname}~ - This will name your pet.

**Beware**, you can only name a pet once. Try looking at your pet to get some quick information about their wellbeing.
