# Help for ~peep~ (skill)

The ~peep~ skill gives special information about NPC's and players.

As you level up the ~peep~ skill, new information becomes available and may become passively useful.

## Usage:

(Lvl 1) You always can tell how healthy an NPC is.  
(Lvl 2) ~peep [player/mob]~ Reveals detailed stats of the player or mob.  
(Lvl 3) ~peep [player/mob]~ Also reveals equipment and items.  
(Lvl 4) ~peep [player/mob]~ Also reveals the chance of dropping equipment.
