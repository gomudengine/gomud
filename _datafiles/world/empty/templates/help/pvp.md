# Help for ~pvp~

PVP is short for Player-versus-player. It's when two or more players can or do enter combat with eachother.

You can see whether PVP is enabled on this server by typing: ~pvp~

The possible settings are:

    **disabled** - PVP is **disabled** on this server. You cannot fight other players.  
    **enabled** - PVP is **enabled** on this server. You can fight other players anywhere.  
    **limited** - PVP is **limited** on this server. You can fight other players in places labeled with: **☠ PK Area ☠**.
