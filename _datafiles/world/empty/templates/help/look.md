# Help for ~look~

The ~look~ command looks at things in the room around you.

## Usage:

  ~look~  
  This looks at the room you are in and tells you about it.

  ~look north~  
  This tries to peer into the room to the north exit.

  ~look dave~  
  This looks at the user **dave** and tells you what you can see about him.

  ~look sword#2~  
  This looks at the second *sword* in your backpack and gives you a description.

  Sometimes there is text in a room description that warrants a closer look.  
  You can often look at these for more information about them.
