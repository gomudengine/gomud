# Help for ~equip~

The ~equip~ command wields or wears an item from your backpack.

## Usage:

  ~equip stick~  
  This would wield a stick from your backpack.  
  ~equip cloak~  
  This would wear a cloak from your backpack.

Aliases: ~wield~, ~wear~

Find out more about referring to items by name by typing ~help item-names~.
