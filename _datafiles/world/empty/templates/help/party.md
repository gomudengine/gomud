# Help for ~party~

The ~party~ manages player parties/groups.

## Usage:

  ~party~ - Lists your current party and its members  
  ~party [create/new/start]~ - Creates a new party that you lead  
  ~party invite [name]~ - Invites a player to your party  
  ~party [accept/join]~ - Accepts an invitation to a party  
  ~party decline~ - Declines an invitation to a party  
  ~party [leave/quit]~ - Leaves a party you are a member of  
  ~party [disband/stop]~ - Destroys a party you lead  
  ~party kick [name]~ - Kicks a player out of a party you lead  
  ~party promote [name]~ - Promotes a player to leader of the party  
  ~party [say/chat] [message]~ - Sends a message only your party can receive  
  ~party autoattack [on/off]~ - Automatically join your party leader in combat
