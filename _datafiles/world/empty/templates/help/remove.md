# Help for ~remove~

The ~remove~ command removes an equipped item and returns it to your backpack.

## Usage:

  ~remove stick~  
  This would remove a stick from your hand and put it in your backpack.  
  ~remove cloak~  
  This would remove a cloak from your body and put it in your backpack.

Aliases: ~unwear~, ~unwield~, ~unequip~

Find out more about referring to items by name by typing ~help item-names~.
