# Help for ~portal~ (skill)

The ~portal~ skills transports you to a new location.

As you level up the ~portal~ skill, it can do more things.

## Usage:

(Lvl 1) ~portal~ Transports you back to the Town Square of Frostfang.  
(Lvl 2) ~portal~ Transports you to the root area of your zone.  
(Lvl 3) ~portal set/clear~ Sets to your current area (or removes) the new location you transport to.  
(Lvl 4) ~portal open~ Creates a physical portal in the room for anyone to travel through.

The lifespan in seconds of your portals is calculated as follows: **Mysticism * 10 + 30**
