# Help for ~map~ (skill)

The ~map~ command displays a map of the area, centered on your character.

Not everything is guaranteed to be shown on the map. For example, hidden areas may not be shown.

With each level of training, your mapping skill will become more effective, covering more area.

## Usage:

(Lvl 1) ~map~ Map a 5x5 area around you.  
(Lvl 2) ~map~ Map a 9x7 area around you.  
(Lvl 3) ~map~ Map a 13x9 area around you.  
(Lvl 4) ~map [wide]~ Map a 17x9 area around you, or if "wide" is specified, twice the distance zoomed out.

Your maps continue to expand with your perception: **Perception/5**
